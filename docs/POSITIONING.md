# Site positioning

**Last updated:** 2026-06-18

## Two sites, one platform

| | **ai-transformation.io** | **ai-transformation.org** |
|--|--------------------------|---------------------------|
| **App** | `web-io` | `web-org` |
| **Audience** | Corporate / enterprise leaders | Community practitioners |
| **Core idea** | Organized AI transformation knowledge | **Learn together** — share experiences |
| **Future** | Possible consultancy product hook (TBD) | Community contributions, peer learning |

## UX (confirmed)

- Default: **light** theme with **dark mode toggle**
- **English only** at launch

## Voice

**web-io:** Authoritative, frameworks, anti-hype, editorial  
**web-org:** Open, collaborative, experience-driven, warmer tone
